When in drought, we bring you lawn-and-order

pHLow delivers a smart sprinkler system that decreases the environmental impact of water waste and lowers landscaping costs for the average homeowner.

Problem

2013 closed as the driest year in recorded history for many areas of California, and current conditions suggest no change in sight for 2014. It's time for residents of California to be conscious of water waste.

Solution

Let pHLow help you lower your landscaping costs and lessen your environmental impact by decreasing water waste.

How It Works

Sensors. Machine learning. Predictive weather data. When you buy a GuardN System you are getting the most advanced system on the market. A host of sensors constantly relay info about the state of your garden to your controller, which analyzes the data for trends. This info combined with predictive weather data, allows the system to create a schedule that saves you water and keeps your garden green!
